This page breaks down how to connect to the Thomas family pictures and home videos server.

This server is used for backup purposes. All pictures are redundantly stored on multiple hard drives so data can be recovered in the event of a hard drive failure. Additionally, pictures stored on this server are backed up to my plex server computer, so that in the event of a natural disaster or a basement flood, the pictures can be recovered from the off-site backup machine.

You MUST be connected to the home wifi "Thomashack" in order to connect to this folder share.

Connect from Windows

  1. Open the file explorer
  2. Click network
  3. Click ThomNAS (NAS stands for Network Attached Storage)
  4. You're good to go. Under no circumstances should you delete pictures from this share unless you are 100% positive you never want to see them again. This folder share is the source of truth for our family pictures. Do not delete/edit or alter any pictures that are not your own.

Connect from macOS (an apple computer)

  1. Open finder
  2. Click network
  3. Click ThomNAS
  4. You're good to go. Under no circumstances should you delete pictures from this share unless you are 100% positive you never want to see them again. This folder share is the source of truth for our family pictures. Do not delete/edit or alter any pictures that are not your own.
